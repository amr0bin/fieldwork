# 24-Week Map

The four-phase arc — Foundations, Literature, Investigation, Synthesis

Each curriculum week generates two posts published one per week — Part 1 the first week, Part 2 the following week. This pacing is intentional: it gives readers time to encounter an idea before being invited to act on it.

## How to navigate the week pages

Each week page contains:
- The heading and its three subheadings
- The three to five keywords for that week
- The key sentence per subheading (the load-bearing claim)
- The pillar tag
- The Part 1 and Part 2 brief (what the posts will argue and do)
- Links to relevant concept pages
