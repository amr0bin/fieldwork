# Case Study Archive

*Real-world examples examined across the 24-week inquiry*

Each week's Part 1 post examines one real-world case — a program, organisation, or practitioner that encountered the week's core challenge and made specific, documented design choices in response. This archive holds the working notes behind those cases.

## How cases are selected

Cases are chosen based on:
- Specificity — enough detail to show the actual choices made, not just the outcome
- Honesty — includes what didn't work or what remained unresolved
- Relevance — directly engages the week's concept and the Fieldwork hypothesis
- Access — the researcher can find sufficient documentation or first-hand account
